Astro Symposium

Astro Symposium is a cosmic journey featuring renowned ISRO scientists and TEDx speakers, offering workshops, conferences, and interactive experiences to ignite young minds with the wonders of the universe.

Celestial Dive

Celestial Dive offers an immersive astronomical experience. Participants delve into celestial wonders through informative workshops and hands-on stargazing sessions equipped with state-of-the-art telescopes.

Cosmic Walk

Cosmic Walk, an evening under the stars, igniting passion for astronomy. Participants embarked on a journey through constellations, capturing the cosmos through astrophotography.

Sky Forge

The workshop empowered participants to design their own rockets using Open Rocket software and provided a platform for aspiring engineers to explore the intricacies of rocket science.

Celestia

A full-day workshop on Celestia, a powerful open source astronomy simulation software. Attendees designed their own cosmic scenarios, delved into data analysis and learnt about customizing Celestia.

Cosmic Time Machine

The workshop gave an insight on the exploration of the universe using the James Webb Space Telescope including its remarkable 'aerospace origami' design and it acting as a Cosmic time machine!
